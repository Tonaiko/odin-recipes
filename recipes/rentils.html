Rice and lentils

A rice and lentils recipe that uses jasmine rice, green lentils, and fied onions

Ingredients

  • 500g jasmine rice
  • 500g green lentils
  • 2 brown onions
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Add lentils to 4 litres of cold water and bring to boil, then set to simmer
  2. Add rice to rice cooker with appropriate amount of water and turn cooker on
  3. Add oil to frying pan and set to medium heat on stove
  4. Chop onions and add to frying pan
